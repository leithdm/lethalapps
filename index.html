Project Management

Our proven agile process produces results

  • Idea

    It doesn’t matter whether you have a clear idea for your app or you need help thinking it through. The idea phase begins with a discovery workshop, where we brainstorm with you on the problem you are trying to solve via your application.

  • Design

    Once we have gathered all the relevant information, our design team sketch ideas and will produce a set of prototype wireframes. We iterate on the wireframes until you are 100% satisfied with the approach before moving onto the development phase.

  • Develop

    Development is where your app is actually built. From the outset, you will be assigned a project manager. Your project manager will be your main point of contact throughout the entire project life-cycle. During this phase, the UX wireframes are turned into a set of fully developed user interface designs.

  • Support

    Once development of your app is complete, we recommend an ongoing maintenance and support agreement in order to extend the life of your app. Our ongoing support can include preventative maintenance or reactive changes based on latest trends. The maintenance and support package can be tailored 100% to reflect your business requirements.
