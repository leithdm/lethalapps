Project Management

Our proven agile process produces results

  • Idea

    It doesn’t matter whether you have a clear idea for your app or you need help thinking it through. The idea phase begins with a discovery workshop where we brainstorm with you on the problem you are trying to solve within your app.

  • Design

    Once all the relevant information is gathered our designers sketch ideas and produce a set of wireframes. They iterate on the wireframes until you are 100% happy with the approach before moving onto our development phase.

  • Develop

    Phase 3 is where your app is built. At the outset, you are assigned a project manager. Your project manager will be your primary point of contact throughout the project. During the development phase, the user experience wireframes are turned into a set of full-fledged user interface designs.

  • Support

    Once development of your app is complete, an ongoing maintenance and support agreement is recommended to extend the life of your app. Our support can include preventative maintenance or reactive changes. The maintenance and support package can be tailored to reflect your business needs.
